Fitness for Everyone

Boost your strength, flexibility, and stamina at home – no gym required.

  • 🏋️ 10-Minute Daily Routine: Stretching + bodyweight squats + push-ups + planks.
  • 🧘 Try Yoga: Simple poses like Cat-Cow, Child’s Pose, or Sun Salutations help flexibility.
  • 🚶 Take Short Walks: 15–30 minute walks after meals improve digestion and mood.
  • 🕒 Be Consistent: Small steps every day are better than intense workouts once a week.
  • 🎧 Music Motivation: Move to your favorite playlist to make workouts enjoyable.
